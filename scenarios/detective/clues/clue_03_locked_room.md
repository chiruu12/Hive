# Clue: The Locked Room

The Restoration Room has one door (deadbolted from inside) and one small ventilation window (6x6 inches) connecting to the kitchen. The deadbolt key was in Marcus's trouser pocket.

The ventilation grate on the kitchen side shows fresh scratches. Two of the four screws are finger-tight, not fully tightened.

A thin rubber tube (3 feet long) is found behind the kitchen shelving unit, near the vent.
