# Clue: The Champagne

The half-empty glass beside Marcus contains champagne with trace amounts of hydrogen cyanide. The bitter almond smell is faint but present.

The champagne bottle on the work table is clean — no poison. The poison was added directly to the glass, not the bottle.

A second clean glass sits on a shelf near the ventilation window. It has Petra's fingerprints on it.

The caterers served champagne in identical glasses. Petra personally delivered a "special toast" glass to Marcus at 10:15 PM, according to a waiter.
