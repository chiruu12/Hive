# Clue: The Insurance Policy

Marcus had a $2 million life insurance policy. The sole beneficiary is his estranged brother, Thomas Thornfield, who lives in another country and was not at the gala.

Diana knew about the insurance policy — she mentioned it to a guest at the gala, saying "Marcus is worth more dead than alive with that policy."

This is a red herring. Diana's comment was bitter sarcasm about the gallery sale, not a murder motive.
