# Clue: Leo's Alibi

Leo claims he left at 10 PM. But security camera footage shows his car in the parking lot until 11:30 PM.

A bartender remembers serving Leo two drinks around 10:15 PM.

Leo's workshop locker contains a bottle of potassium cyanide — standard for certain art restoration processes. The bottle is half-empty. Leo says he's been using it for months on a Victorian-era piece.

Leo's phone records show a 4-minute call to Diana at 9:50 PM, just after her argument with Marcus.
