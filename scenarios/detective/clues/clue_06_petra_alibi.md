# Clue: Petra's Alibi

Kitchen staff confirm Petra was managing the kitchen most of the evening. However, two staff members took a smoke break from 10:30 PM to 10:50 PM, leaving Petra alone in the kitchen.

Petra's catering van contains industrial cleaning supplies, including a bottle labeled "almond extract" that smells unusually strong.

A kitchen staff member noticed Petra washing a rubber tube in the sink around 11 PM. Petra said it was "for the chocolate fountain."

The kitchen had no chocolate fountain.
